
Jeddah Corniche Circuit - Surface Detection AR Kit
==================================================

How to use:
-----------
1. Upload `index.html` to the root of your GitHub repo: Jeddah-Corniche-Circuit
2. Enable GitHub Pages (Settings > Pages > Branch: main > Folder: / (root)).
3. Visit: https://ElCarlositoo.github.io/Jeddah-Corniche-Circuit/
4. Open on your phone, allow camera permissions.
5. Move device to scan surfaces; tap to place the circuit.

QR Code:
---------
Scan qr.png or QR_Print_Card.png to open directly.

Future QR updates:
------------------
- Edit `regenerate_qr.py` to change the TARGET_URL.
- Run: python regenerate_qr.py
- Commit the new qr.png and QR_Print_Card.png

Enjoy your AR experience!
